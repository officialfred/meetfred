About this project

This webmap is part of my capstone project, where I analysed the underwhelming deployment of LinkNYC kiosks in the outter boroughs of New York City. The main purpose of this map is to outline not only how shy the deployment of LinkNYC has been in the outter boroughs, but also to highlight the stagnation of the program, thus the choice of a timeline.
